Our Story

Authentic Coastal Flavors, Crafted with Passion.

Located in the heart of the historic coastal town, Al-Ustad Hotel is recognized as the top destination for seafood lovers in Trivandrum. We pride ourselves on sourcing the freshest fish directly from the Vizhinjam harbor every single morning.

Whether you are craving a fiery Neymeen roast or the traditional Karimeen Pollichathu, our chefs prepare every dish using freshly ground, authentic Kerala spices.
